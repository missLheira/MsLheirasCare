What You Have To Know About Me

With over a decade of dedicated experience, I am a compassionate nanny with expertise in caring for children from infancy through adolescence. My journey has taken me from Los Angeles to Dallas, Texas, supporting families with newborns, including those facing special needs such as colic, reflux, gas, tongue-tie, and lip-tie.


As a mother of four, I have a deep understanding of the nuances of childcare, and I focus on creating a nurturing environment. My specialty lies in newborn care, and I am certified in sleep training with further certification as a sleep consultant. I am CPR-certified and currently pursuing my Newborn Care Specialist degree to strengthen my skills even further. I strongly believe in the importance of routines and draw from various training programs to implement effective childcare strategies. My experience includes working with twins, and I have also managed household responsibilities, organizing nanny schedules, and hiring nannies for families.

After five years of nannying, I earned a degree in business management and served as a director of operations overseeing over 20 stores in Dallas. Ultimately, I returned to nannying, driven by my genuine passion for working with families and children.
